🤝 Ways You Can Help

🎷 Donate Instruments

We welcome donations of used or new instruments. Your gift will disciple and inspire young girls through music.

  • Local Donors (Kenya): Deliver to Kitale, Trans Nzoia County.
  • International Donors: We seek partners to coordinate pickup and shipping.

🚚 Help Us Ship

We need logistics partners to ship instruments to Kenya free of charge. Your support will expand access to girls who may never otherwise touch an instrument.

🏫 Partner With Us

We invite churches, schools, families, and companies to join us in equipping and delivering instruments.

💛 Give Financially

If you don’t have an instrument, consider giving financially to purchase one. Every contribution disciples and uplifts.
