🌟 Welcome to Cross & Crown Ministries

Here at Cross & Crown, we believe every child is radiant, gifted, and called. We walk with them—through discipleship, creativity, and compassion—so they can rise in faith and flourish in purpose.

Whether you're a mentor, a parent, a partner, or a seeker, you've found a place where hearts are nurtured, stories are honored, and Christ is at the center of it all.

Together, we build. Together, we protect. Together, we shine.

We value your feedback, input, and suggestions. Feel free to write to us—we’ll be glad to reach out and walk with you. You can use the contact form on this site, or write to us directly at info@crossandcrown.org

God bless you.

✨ Message from the Founder

“Cross & Crown was born from a deep conviction: that every child deserves to be seen, protected, and empowered through faith. What began as a whisper from God became a movement—one rooted in discipleship, creativity, and compassion. I’ve watched girls rise in courage, mentors grow in grace, and communities transform through simple acts of walking together. I have witnessed children born in the slums transform, excel and become change makers in their own families and communities. I believe every child carries spiritual capacity far beyond what the world often sees. They are capable of deep faith, radiant creativity, and powerful leadership when nurtured with love and truth. This ministry exists to walk with them—to affirm their gifts, protect their stories, and disciple them into the fullness of who God created them to be. Whether through mentorship, sports, music, storytelling, or compassion, we create spaces where children rise in courage and shine in purpose. And I believe every girl—regardless of her background, status, or ability—has the potential to become a great leader and a bold change-maker in her community. When we walk with her, affirm her gifts, and disciple her with compassion, we unlock something eternal. If you’re here, you’re part of that story. Please reach out to us, tell us which pillar you align to, support us in any way:

  • Drop us an encouragement note.
  • Pray with us.
  • Donate to our ministry. No amount is small.
  • Go (become a mentor / discipler / volunteer teacher or trainer). We welcome you all.
  • Sponsor one child or more.

In any way possible, you have a space in our home. We welcome you with love.

Emmanuel Godfrey Ogoma (Founder)

✨ Core Values of Cross & Crown Ministries

A ministry rooted in Christ, creativity, and compassion.

Christ-Centered Living

We walk daily with Jesus, anchoring every action in faith, truth, and love.

🌟 Core Value 1: Christ-Centred Living

💬 “Jesus First, Always!”

Jesus is our number one! We follow Him in everything — at home, at school, in church, and with friends. We want our words, actions, and choices to show that Jesus lives in us.

📖 Colossians 3:17 — "And whatever you do, whether in word or deed, do it all in the name of the Lord Jesus, giving thanks to God the Father through him."

🎯 Challenge: This week, before making any decision, stop and ask: “What would Jesus want me to do?” Then do it with a thankful heart.

Empowering Relationships

We build trusted mentorships that equip young hearts with wisdom, dignity, and practical tools.

🌟 Core Value 2: Empowering Relationships

💬 “We Build Each Other Up!”

We choose to be kind, encouraging, and helpful to each other. We build friendships that make us stronger in faith and life, and we cheer each other on to become who God made us to be.

📖 1 Thessalonians 5:11 — "Therefore encourage one another and build each other up, just as in fact you are doing."

🎯 Challenge: This week, say one kind or encouraging thing to a friend or family member every day — and watch how it brightens their day!

Transformative Storytelling

We share God’s Word through creative narratives that shape identity, purpose, and hope.

🌟 Core Value 3: Transformative Storytelling

💬 “Stories That Change Lives!”

We use stories from the Bible and real life to inspire, teach, and transform hearts. Every story is a chance to learn about God’s love, make wise choices, and become the person God wants us to be.

📖 Deuteronomy 6:6-7 — "These commandments that I give you today are to be on your hearts. Impress them on your children..."

🎯 Challenge: This week, share a Bible story or a lesson you’ve learned with a friend or family member — and talk about how it can change your actions or choices.

Creative Expression

We celebrate music, movement, and art as reflections of divine beauty and joy.

🌟 Core Value 4: Creative Expression

💬 “Serving Others, Serving Jesus!”

We show God’s love by helping and caring for others, especially those in need. Every small act of kindness makes a big difference and reflects Jesus’ heart to the world.

📖 1 Peter 4:10 — "Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others, faithfully administering God’s grace in its various forms."

🎯 Challenge: This week, do one kind act for someone without expecting anything in return — help a friend, family member, or neighbour in need.

Compassionate Service

We live out Christ’s love through empathy, justice, and community care.

🌟 Core Value 5: Compassionate Service

💬 “Serving Others, Serving Jesus!”

We show God’s love by helping and caring for others, especially those in need. Every small act of kindness makes a big difference and reflects Jesus’ heart to the world.

📖 1 Peter 4:10 — "Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others, faithfully administering God’s grace in its various forms."

🎯 Challenge: This week, do one kind act for someone without expecting anything in return — help a friend, family member, or neighbour in need.

Unity in Diversity

We honor every child’s voice, background, and calling.

🌟 Core Value 6: Unity in Diversity

💬 “One Body, Many Parts.”

We celebrate that God made each of us unique — with different backgrounds, talents, and ideas. By respecting and learning from one another, we grow stronger together and reflect God’s love in our diversity.

📖 1 Corinthians 12:12 — "There is one body, but it has many parts. But all its many parts make up one body. It is the same with Christ."

🎯 Challenge: This week, get to know someone different from you — learn about their interests, culture, or talents, and celebrate what makes them special.

Excellence with Grace

We pursue high standards guided by grace, humility, and spiritual clarity.

🌟 Core Value 7: Excellence with Grace

💬 “Excellence That Reflects Jesus.”

We strive to do our best in everything — learning, serving, and creating — while treating others with kindness, patience, and humility. Our goal is to honour God through both our effort and our attitude.

📖 Colossians 3:23 — "Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord, not for human masters."

🎯 Challenge: This week, give your best effort in one task — schoolwork, ministry, or a creative project — and do it with a kind and humble heart toward others.

Spiritual Capacity of Children

We believe children can know God, hear His voice, and live out their faith with boldness and joy.

🌟 Core Value 8: Spiritual Capacity of Children

💬 “Little Hearts, Big Faith.”

We believe every child has the ability to know, love, and follow God. By nurturing their faith, teaching them God’s Word, and encouraging them to use their gifts, we help children grow into strong, Spirit-filled followers of Jesus.

📖 Mark 10:14 — "Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of God belongs to such as these."

🎯 Challenge: This week, spend time learning a new Bible verse, pray daily, or share what you learned about God with a friend or family member.
